The Best Movies According to Hugh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Intersteller

This is my favourite movie. I love the story and concept of space.

Moneyball

Really cool movie about data science and how it can be applied to seek out players in baseball.

The Accountant

The storyline follows Christian Wolff, a certified public accountant with autism. He makes his living (un-)'cooking the books' of criminal and terrorist organizations around the world that are experiencing internal embezzlement.
